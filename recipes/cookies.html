Basic cookies

Ingredients

  • 225g of butter
  • 110g of caster sugar
  • 275g of plain flour
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 75g of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 190C and cream the butter in a large bowl
  2. Add sugar and keep beating until mix is light and fluffy
  3. Sift in the flour and add in the chocolate chips
  4. Bring the mixture together with your hands in a figure-of-eight motion to form the dough
  5. Roll the dough into walnut sized balls
  6. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until golden brown and allow to cool
