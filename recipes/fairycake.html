Fairycake

Basic fairycakes

Ingredients

  • 110g butter or margerine
  • 110g of caster sugar
  • 2 free range eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 110g self-raising flour
  • 1-2 tbsp milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C and line 2 x 12-hole fairy cake tins with paper cases
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in a bowl until pale. Beat in the eggs, a little at a time, and stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Fold the flour using large metal spoon, add milk until the mixture is soft and add mixture into papaer cases
  4. Bake in the ovem for 8-10 minutes. Set aside to cool for 10 minutes, then remove from the tin and cool on wire rack
