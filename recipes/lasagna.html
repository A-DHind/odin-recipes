Lasagna

A delicious homemade Lasagna dish

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of good tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces of tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped oregano
  • Salt
  • 1/2 pound of dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 pound of grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Let thsi simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheese ready
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
